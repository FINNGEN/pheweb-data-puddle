About this site


The genetic association results on this website are from the FinnGen study. These results are from 2,861 endpoints of data freeze 6 (autumn 2020), consisting of 260,405 individuals.

This site was built with PheWeb (original repository, Finngen repository). All positions are on GRCh38.
